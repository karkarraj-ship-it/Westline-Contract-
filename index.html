About Us

Westline Contractors Pty Ltd is a trusted telecommunications subcontractor delivering fibre and copper network services across Western Australia. We specialize in FTTP activations, copper remediation, and fibre network upgrades, combining accredited skills, industry experience, and a strong safety culture.

With direct delivery experience under major partners like Ventia, Service Stream, and Unify, we ensure every project meets nbn® standards while exceeding client expectations. Our team is equipped with industry-approved test gear, splicing kits, and civil tools for end-to-end service.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive telecommunications services to support Australia’s fibre and copper networks.

Fibre Services

  • Lead-in installation (underground, aerial, direct bury)
  • Fibre splicing and jointing (CommScope, Corning, Madison)
  • PCD & NTD installation and relocation
  • Optical testing (OTDR, OPM, ILM, VFL)
  • Fibre remediation and fault resolution

Copper Services

  • ONX-580 diagnostics (SELT, TDR, HLOG)
  • Pair swaps and jumper management
  • Fault resolution (water ingress, corrosion)
  • Temporary cable installations

Civil & Site Works

  • Pit and pipe installation
  • Rod, rope, and cable hauling
  • Direct bury fibre installations
  • Driveway crossings and trenching

Testing & Assurance

  • VDSL/fibre sync testing
  • End-to-end activation testing
  • Compliance with partner systems
